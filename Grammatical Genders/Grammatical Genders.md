All German [[Nouns]] belong to one of three Grammatical Genders:
- [[Masculine]]
- [[Feminine]]
- [[Neuter]]

| gender    | [[Definite Articles]] | [[Indefinite Articles]] |
| --------- | --------------------- | ----------------------- |
| masculine | der Mann              | ein Mann                |
| neuter    | das Baby              | ein Baby                |
| feminine  | die Frau              | eine Frau               |
