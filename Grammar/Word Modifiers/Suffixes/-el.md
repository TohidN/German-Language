suffix in diminutives
> [!example] 
> - ‎==Kind== (child) + ==‎-el== → ‎==die Kindel== (little child, darling)
> - ‎==Haus== (house) + ==‎-el== → ‎==die Häusel== (little house)

suffix in nouns indicating appurtenance
> [!example] 
> - ‎==Arm== (arm) + ==‎-el== → ‎==die Ärmel== (sleeve)
> - ‎==Eiche== (oak) + ==‎-el== → ‎==die Eichel== (acorn)
