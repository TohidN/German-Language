# Possessive & Genitive Pronouns
| Nominative | Possessive  |  Translation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ich | mein | my |
| du | dein | your |
| er | sein | his |
| sie | ihr | her |
| es | sein | its |
| wir | unser | our |
| ihr | euer | your |
| sie | ihr | their |
